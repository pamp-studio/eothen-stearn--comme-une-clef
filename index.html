This interview is between artist Eothen Stearn, and the organisation Maison des femme in Montreuil, France.
It was made in passing in January 2020 before the crux of the pandemic.
Special thanks to des Maison femme for their generosity, openness and approachability.
Spaces like these serve as a lifeline to women and womxn. This resilience and ongoing activism is crucial with the rise of domestic abuse on a global scale during lockdown. They offer ongoing support to vulnerable individuals and communities.
Thank you La MdF for your work, your words, your retrospective and future life. It was a pleasure! I hope to see you in the future under safer circumstances.

Audience members go visit or get in contact safely with these brilliant people: Maison des Femme

Comme une clef is part of a commission for exhibition, La Clinque du Queer at Maison Populaire
Show runs 26 september - 11 december 2020.
Curated by Thomas Conchou.
Text transcribed/translated by Aurélien Potier.
